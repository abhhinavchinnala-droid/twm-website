What this is

Not another room.
A different standard.

TWM is a community-building initiative operating under Margam — a Warangal-based organization that works closely with students and professionals on education, career direction, and long-term outcomes.

The standard is uncompromising: genuine value for every member. No noise. No self-promotion theater. Just people building things, together.
